Meissner扩大厂房通知

2014年02月13日

Change Notification

      Meissner Filtration Products will be expanding its operations to include a 176,000 ft2 building located at 1001 Flynn Rd, Camarillo. This state of the art facility will support continuous growth both in terms production capacity and support services, and is conveniently located a half mile from our current manufacturing facilities. Our current ISO Class 7 cleanroom manufacturing operations will be relocated to the new facility, providing 40,000 ft2 of cleanroom space. This notification affects all filters and single-use systems. The new ISO Class 7 cleanroom facilities were designed according to ISO 14644, incorporate our own high quality standards, and will fully comply with cGMP guidelines. Please note that there will be no changes to manufacturing equipment, processes, products, packaging, labeling, specifications, drawings or certification material. Completion and commissioning of the new cleanroom manufacturing space is scheduled for March 2014. Commissioning activities will include, but are not limited to the following: a thorough analysis of both viable and non-viable particulate, verification of air flow and positive pressurization, and qualification of the control and monitoring systems. Pending a successful commissioning, the cleanroom manufacturing space will be put into service in April 2014. A final certification of the new cleanroom manufacturing space will occur immediately following this to verify that it continues to function as designed during peak operation. We are confident that our comprehensive planning will allow for a smooth transition into the new facility without disruption to our customers. For example, customer service will remain available during the transition, and email, phone, and fax will not be affected. Meissner is proud of this new facility and will welcome customer visits, and quality audits as appropriate, beginning June 1, 2014. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us at QA@Meissner.com.
